ABOUT US

At HATCX, we believe the healthcare industry needs to changeand we want to be that positive change. We are bursting with ideas, energy, and a deep understanding of how healthcare works (and often, how it doesnt). We exist to help connect you with your health.

We believe in creating easy, simple solutions to a complex industry. We want to empower you with the right tools to make the best personal health decisions. We want to innovate where innovation is needed mostto bring transparency to service, give you control of your personal health information and make it easy to access.

Ultimately we want to change the way you think about and use healthcare.
